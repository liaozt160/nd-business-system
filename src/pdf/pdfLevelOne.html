BUSINESS DESCRIPTION

For over 65 years this specialty manufacturing business has focused on the aerospace and medical industries to provide high quality metal components. This well-established company has highly skilled labor, extensive record keeping and excellent brand equity with customers. Products and quality are produced with AS9100D and ISO9001:2015 certified systems. The parts are fabricated from almost every metal and alloy, which range from simple through complicated forms. The shop specializes in; Deep drawing, Hydroforming, Precision stamping and Metal forming.
